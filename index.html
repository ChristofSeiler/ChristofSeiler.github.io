I am an assistant professor of statistics at the Department of Advanced Computing Sciences in Maastricht (the Netherlands), and I am building a research group in biomedical data science at the Center of Experimental Rheumatology in Zurich (Switzerland).

I earned my PhD in Bern (Switzerland) and Sophia Antipolis (France) advised by Mauricio Reyes and Xavier Pennec. I did my postdoc at Stanford University advised by Susan Holmes at the Department of Statistics, and we closely collaborated with Catherine Blish and her lab in immunology.
