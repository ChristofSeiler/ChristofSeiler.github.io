Bio: I earned my PhD in computer science and biomedical engineering in Switzerland and France. Together with my advisors, Xavier Pennec and Mauricio Reyes, we worked on medical image registration algorithms. Then, I moved to the Department of Statistics at Stanford University as a postdoctoral fellow and later as a research scientist. Together with my advisor, Susan Holmes, we worked on a wide range of topics in mathematical and applied statistics. Together with Catherine Blish and her lab, we developed the R package CytoGLMM. Now, I am building my own research group in statistics at the Department of Advanced Computing Sciences in the Netherlands.
